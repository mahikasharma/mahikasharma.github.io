About Me

Hello! My name is Mahika Sharma (she/her), and I'm from the Bay Area in California, where I have lived my whole life. I am currently a 3rd year junior student at Northeastern University in Boston, where I am studying computer science with a concentration in AI. My goal with my degree is to pursue research and/or a PhD in bioinformatics or computational physics.

My interests in pursuing this research stemmed from two lab experiences since entering Northeastern. I have gained experience in computational biomechanics at Northeastern, where I worked with physics simulations regarding the effects of collisions on the brain. I am currently a research assistant at the Czech Academy of Sciences, focusing on database development and data visualization for parasitic relationships.

In addition to my research work, I recently completed my first co-op in the fall as a data engineer at Purple Carrot, a meal kit company in greater Boston. Overall, I hope to take my career in the direction of data and computational sciences. I would also be interested in being a professor someday and continue research projects.

Outside of academics, my hobbies include playing video games, music (I play the flute and ukulele), and reading fictional books.
